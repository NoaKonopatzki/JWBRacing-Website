General

JWB Racing is a SimRacing team from Germany, established in 2021,
which has already proved successful in several races and championships.
JWB Racing is a young team that has many opportunities for further
development. Especially for beginners, JWB Racing is the perfect
introduction to the diverse world of SimRacing, as we train and
advance new drivers together with experienced driver to get the
best and more out of each member. Thanks to both young and experienced
members, JWB Racing offers a sufficient environment of opportunities.
Team spirit and cohesion among the members are paramount at JWB
Racing in order to ensure an optimal atmosphere among themselves.
JWB Racing offers seats not only for drivers, but also for creative
design and social media content, which contributes to the diversity of the team.
