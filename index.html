General

JWB Racing is a SimRacing team from Germany, established in 2021,
who has already proved them selfs successful in several races and championships.
JWB Racing is a young team that has many opportunities for further development.
Especially for beginners, JWB Racing is the perfect introduction to the diverse
world of SimRacing, as we train and advance new drivers together with experienced
drivers to get the best and more out of each member. Thanks to both young and
experienced members, JWB Racing offers a sufficient environment of opportunities.
Team spirit and cohesion among the members are paramount at JWB Racing in order to
ensure an optimal atmosphere among themselves. JWB Racing offers seats not only for
drivers, but also for creative design and social media content, which contributes
to the diversity of the excersises for the members.
